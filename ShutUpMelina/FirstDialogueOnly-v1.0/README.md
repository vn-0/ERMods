What this mod does:
- This mod skips the dialogue between the player and Melina that gives the player the Spectral Steed Whistle for summoning Torrent.
- When you first sit down at a Site of Grace that would normally start Melina's cutscene, you will instead immediately receive the Spectral Steed Whistle after watching or skipping her introductory cutscene. 
- Due to the changes made, the game will already consider you to have made the accord, meaning that you will be at the bonfire menu immediately after sitting down down and can level up with Melina.

What this mod doesn't do:
- This mod does not skip the cutscene that appears when you first sit down at a Site of Grace that triggers Melina's appearance.
- This mod makes no adjustments to Melina's other appearances, cutscenes or dialogue triggers.
- This mod makes no changes to the Site of Grace requirements needed to first have Melina appear.
- This mod does not give you the Spectral Steed Whistle right away. Instead, you will have to progress like usual to receive it.

Useful information for initial progression:
- It is my current understanding that, to reach a Site of Grace where Melina can spawn, you must first have at least two other Sites of Grace touched. This can be anywhere on the map.
- The following Sites of Grace will trigger Melina to appear (and play her introductory cutscene in vanilla):
1. Limgrave - Church of Elleh
2. Limgrave - Agheel Lake North
3. Limgrave - Third Church of Marika
4. Limgrave - Stormhill Shack
5. Limgrave - Agheel Lake South
6. Limgrave - Gatefront Ruins
7. Weeping Peninsula - Bridge of Sacrifice
8. Limgrave - Seaside Ruins
- Melina will not appear at any other Site of Grace. She will not appear at any of these eight listed Sites of Grace either if you have not already touched at least two other Sites of Grace.

How to install this mod for me³:
- Open the Elden Ring mod profile you intend to use for this mod.
- Copy the "script" folder into this profile folder or any subdirectory you have created.
- Ensure that your profile is correctly configured to read files from within these folders. me³ might not read files from these folders if you have configured your profile to read files from a subdirectory and these folders are not located within that subdirectory.
- Start Elden Ring through your me³ mod profile.
